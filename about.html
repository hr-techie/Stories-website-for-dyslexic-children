Our Story

We are a team of four friends who came together to make reading easier and more fun for children with dyslexia. Each of us brings our own skills—writing, designing, illustrating, and creating audio—to build stories that are clear, colorful, and full of imagination. We started this website to help every child enjoy reading, no matter how they learn best.


For Parents & Teachers

Our stories are designed to build confidence and joy in children with dyslexia. Use the audio feature, read together, or let kids explore at their own pace.


FAQs

Why are the fonts different?

We use dyslexia-friendly fonts that make letters easier to recognize.

Can children listen instead of reading?

Yes! Every story comes with audio support.
